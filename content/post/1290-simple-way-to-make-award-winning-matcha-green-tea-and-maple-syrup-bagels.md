##### The ingredients needed to make Matcha Green Tea and Maple Syrup Bagels:

1. Make ready 180 grams ◎ Bread (strong) flour
1. Prepare 90 grams ◎ Water
1. Prepare 15 grams ◎ Maple syrup
1. Get 7 grams ◎ Matcha
1. Prepare 2 grams ◎ Salt
1. Take 1/2 tsp ◎ Dry active yeast
1. Prepare 1 tbsp Honey, sugar, or molasses




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Matcha Green Tea and Maple Syrup Bagels:

1. Add the ◎ ingredients into a bowl and mix with your hands. Take the dough out onto a flat surface and knead for about 15 minutes.
1. Divide the dough into 4 pieces and roll them up. Cover with a plastic wrap or a dampened towel and let rest for 10 minutes.
1. Roll it out into a 15 x 7 cm oval shape. Leaving 3 cm of width at the right end, fold in 3 layers and tightly seal the seam.
1. Cover the left end with the 3 cm edge on the right and make it into a doughnut shape. Let rest for 20 minutes at 35℃.
1. Bring plenty of water to a boil in a frying pan, reduce to low heat and add honey. Gently insert the bagels and cook 30 seconds each side.
1. Transfer to a baking sheet, and immediately transfer to a preheated 200℃ oven. Bake for 20 minutes at 180℃ and they&#39;re done.
